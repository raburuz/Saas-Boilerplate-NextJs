Privacy policies and terms of service are mandatory when setting up a Stripe account or applying for Google OAuth consent screen.

1. Navigate to the **../content/legal/** folder
2. Modify the content from **privacy-policy.mdx** to create your own Privacy Policy
2. Modify the content from **terms-of-service.mdx** to create your own Term of Service
