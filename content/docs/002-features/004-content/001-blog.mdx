## Create your Blog Post

* Navigate to the **../content/blog** directory
* Create a new **.mdx** file

``` shell

---
title: Introducing your Application
publishedAt: 2023-10-05
summary: Today, we are excited to launch X platform
image: https://domain.com/image.png
author: Musk
authorImage: https://domain.com/author.png
---

Write your content here

```

Note: The **publishedAt** field determines the order of posts based on their publication dates
